FarmYard

Support Center

Get help with your FarmYard account, listings, and marketplace activity

Quick Help: Check the FAQ section below before contacting support. Most common issues can be resolved in minutes!

Contact Support

Need immediate assistance? We're here to help:

  • Email: support@modernfarmyard.com
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 2 business days
  • Support Hours: Monday–Friday, 9:00 AM–5:30 PM EAT

When contacting support, please include:

  • Your FarmYard username or email address
  • The specific issue or error you're experiencing
  • Steps you've already tried
  • Your device type and app version (if using the mobile app)
  • Any relevant listing or message IDs

Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Login Issues

  • Forgot your password? Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen and follow the email instructions to reset it.
  • Can't receive password reset emails? Check your spam/junk folder or contact support with a different email address on file.
  • How do I enable two-factor authentication? Go to Account Settings → Security and enable 2FA to protect your account.
  • Want to change your email? Go to Account Settings → Email and follow the verification process.
  • How do I delete my account? Go to Account Settings → Delete Account. Your data will be removed within 30 days.

Posting & Listing Issues

  • Why was my listing rejected? Listings must be agriculture-related and follow marketplace guidelines. Check the rejection message for details. You can resubmit after making corrections.
  • How long does it take for a listing to approve? Most listings are approved within 1-2 hours. Some may take up to 24 hours for moderation review.
  • Can I edit a listing after posting? Yes, click the listing and select "Edit". Changes are reviewed before going live.
  • How do I delete a listing? Go to My Listings → select the listing → Delete. It will be removed immediately.
  • What image formats are supported? JPG, PNG, and WebP images up to 10MB. Use high-quality, clear photos of the actual product.
  • Can I post on behalf of someone else? Yes, if you're a verified sales representative of a company. The listing must show your company's information.

Messaging & Calling

  • How do I message another user? Click on a listing and select "Message Seller" or go to Messages → Start Conversation.
  • Can I call other users? Yes, if you have FarmYard installed on a mobile device or using a supported browser, click the phone icon to start a call.
  • Why can't I make a call? Check that microphone is enabled in app permissions and both users have a stable internet connection.
  • Is call data saved? Call history shows timing and duration for safety records, but call audio is not recorded.
  • How do I block a user? Open their profile or chat → More Options → Block User. They won't be able to contact you.
  • Can I delete a conversation? Yes, open the chat → More Options → Delete Chat. This is permanent.

Payments & Transactions

  • Does FarmYard process payments? No. FarmYard is a marketplace for connecting buyers and sellers. All payments are arranged directly between parties outside the app.
  • Is it safe to pay through FarmYard? Never send payments through FarmYard messages. Arrange payment details, location, and timing directly with the other party.
  • What should I do if someone claims payment failed? Verify the transaction with your bank. If it's a FarmYard messaging issue, contact support.
  • How do I request an order? Click on a listing and select "Request Order", specify quantity and delivery details, then discuss terms with the seller.

Verification & Trust

  • What does the Verified Company badge mean? It means the company has passed FarmYard's review process and maintains high marketplace standards.
  • How do I become a verified seller? Email support@modernfarmyard.com with your business registration details, documents, and contact information.
  • How often are companies reverified? Annually, or if there are complaints or policy violations.
  • Can I rate other users? Yes, after a transaction. Open the conversation → Rate User → leave feedback to help other buyers and sellers.

Safety & Reporting

  • I received a suspicious message or offer. Report it immediately by clicking Report User or Report Listing and provide details.
  • What should I do if someone is scamming? Don't send money. Screenshot the conversation and report the user to support@modernfarmyard.com.
  • Is my location information shared? Only your general location for finding nearby listings. Your exact address is not shown unless you choose to share it in messages.
  • How do I report inappropriate content? Click Report → select the reason → submit. Our team reviews reports within 24 hours.

App & Technical Issues

  • The app is slow or crashes. Try: restarting the app, clearing app cache, or reinstalling from the app store.
  • Images won't upload. Check your internet connection, image size (under 10MB), and app permissions for camera/photo access.
  • Notifications aren't working. Go to Settings → Notifications and enable "FarmYard Notifications".
  • My listing isn't showing up. Check that it's approved. Go to My Listings → click the listing to see its status.
  • Dark mode doesn't work. Go to Account tab and click the theme toggle button to switch between light and dark modes.

Report Issues

Safety Issues

If you encounter fraudulent activity, abuse, or safety concerns:

  • Report the listing or user immediately within the app
  • Do not send money or personal information to suspicious users
  • Email support@modernfarmyard.com with details and screenshots
  • We investigate all reports within 24 hours

Listing Moderation Appeals

If your listing was rejected or removed:

  • Check the rejection reason in your notifications
  • Review FarmYard's marketplace rules (see Privacy & Terms in the app)
  • Make corrections and resubmit the listing
  • If you believe the decision was wrong, email support with your listing ID and explanation

Account Issues

  • Locked account? Verify your identity by confirming your phone number or email.
  • Suspended account? You'll receive an email with the reason. Contact support to appeal within 30 days.
  • Hacked account? Change your password immediately and contact support to review suspicious activity.

Marketplace Rules

All users must follow these guidelines:

  • Agriculture-Only: Only post products and services related to farming, agriculture, and food production
  • Honest Listings: Accurately describe products, prices, quantities, and availability
  • Truthful Profiles: Use your real name and accurate company information
  • Respectful Communication: No harassment, spam, or abusive messages
  • No Fraud: Don't misrepresent products, hide defects, or collect payments without delivering goods
  • Legal Compliance: Ensure you have required licenses and permits for your products/services
  • No Stolen Content: Don't use photos or descriptions from other listings

Violations may result in: Listing removal, account suspension, or permanent ban.
