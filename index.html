Privacy, Terms, and Marketplace Rules

Policy and Compliance

Privacy Policy

FarmYard collects information users provide when creating accounts, editing profiles, creating company profiles, posting listings, sending messages, uploading media, saving listings, requesting orders, rating users, reporting issues, or contacting support.

That information may include names, email addresses, phone numbers, profile photos, company details, listing details, uploaded images or videos, chat content, order notes, invite records, ratings, reports, and related account activity needed to operate the marketplace.

FarmYard may require a phone number for account setup, account recovery, trust, and communication features. Users may still choose whether that number is shown publicly on their profile.

FarmYard may also store technical and usage information needed to keep users signed in, remember app state, protect accounts, troubleshoot problems, and improve platform performance.

If users choose to start an in-app call, FarmYard may request microphone permission on the device. Microphone access should only be used for the call experience and should not be used outside the calling feature without clear notice.

If a user hides their phone number, FarmYard should not display that number publicly and browser-based call actions should not expose or use it for web-initiated dialer calls. Hidden-number users may still receive calls through supported in-app calling features.

FarmYard uses collected information to run the marketplace, display listings and profiles, support messaging and calling, manage company-team access, improve safety, investigate abuse, and respond to user requests.

FarmYard should limit collection to what is reasonably necessary, use appropriate security practices, and give users a clear way to request account help, profile changes, or data-related support.

Terms of Use

FarmYard is an agriculture-only marketplace for produce, raw materials, finished agricultural goods, and agricultural services.

Users are responsible for the accuracy of listing titles, prices, quantities, availability, photos, media uploads, delivery details, order information, and contact information shared through the platform.

Users are also responsible for keeping required account information, including a valid phone number, accurate and up to date, even if they choose to hide that number from public profile display.

Users must not post misleading information, impersonate others, interfere with marketplace activity, misuse messaging or calling features, or attempt to gain unauthorized access to another account, company, or sales team.

FarmYard may remove listings, suspend accounts, or restrict access where listings or conduct appear unsafe, misleading, abusive, or outside the purpose of the platform.

Seller Requirements

Listings should clearly state category, product or service name, unit, price, location, and any minimum order or service scope.

Sellers should only list agriculture-related items or services and should describe quality, availability, timing, and delivery terms honestly.

Images should match the listing and must not be stolen, misleading, or unrelated to the item or service being offered.

Where local law requires permits, licenses, inspections, or certifications, sellers are responsible for meeting those obligations before listing or selling.

Prohibited Content and Conduct

FarmYard should not be used for illegal goods, unrelated consumer products, harassment, spam, deceptive pricing, abusive calling or messaging, unsafe transactions, or unauthorized recording or misuse of user media.

Users must not post content that infringes another person’s rights, contains false claims, spreads malware, collects data from other users without permission, or uses company access credentials on behalf of an unauthorized person.

Repeated violations may lead to listing removal, account restrictions, or permanent suspension.

Safety and Support

Users should verify counterparties, confirm price and quantity terms, and use caution before making payments or handing over goods.

FarmYard does not collect, hold, route, or guarantee buyer or seller payments. Any payment arrangement happens outside FarmYard and is the responsibility of the parties involved.

Users should use messaging, media sharing, profile review, ratings, reports, and company verification details as safety tools, but they should still make independent checks before any trade, pickup, delivery, or call-based agreement.

When a phone number is hidden, web users may be limited from starting direct dialer calls to that user. In supported FarmYard app experiences, users may still be able to connect through in-app audio calling without exposing the hidden number to the other party.

For a production version of FarmYard, this section should include a support email, complaint channel, fraud-reporting path, and a clear process for account, listing, team-access, or communication complaints.

This is a product-ready starter policy set, not legal advice. A lawyer should review it before public launch.

Verified Company Term

Only company profiles are eligible for the Verified Company badge. Individual profiles may still use FarmYard and may post on behalf of a company if the company has assigned them as sales representatives.

Each company may assign up to 4 individuals to sell on its behalf, and one assigned individual may act as the company admin with permission to appoint or remove the other representatives. A company may also operate with fewer than 4 assigned individuals.

An individual user may belong to only one company sales team at a time. FarmYard may block invites, approvals, or account access changes that would place the same user in more than one company team at once.

Verification requires more than payment. FarmYard may require business registration documents, a verified company email, a verified company phone number, a confirmed business location, a complete company profile, a clean moderation history, and any permits or certifications required for the goods or services being sold.

A monthly Verified Company subscription of $10 may apply after the company passes review. The subscription supports the verified badge and company trust features, but it does not guarantee sales, ranking priority, or business performance.

FarmYard may remove or suspend the Verified Company badge if a company fails review, submits misleading information, loses required compliance status, violates marketplace rules, or allows unauthorized individuals to operate its listings.

Payments cannot be made through FarmYard. Use the app only to connect, discuss terms, and arrange the transaction directly with the other party.
