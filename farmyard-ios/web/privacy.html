Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how FarmYard collects, uses, stores, and shares information when people use the FarmYard marketplace on web, Android, and iPhone.

Information We Collect

  • Account details such as name, email address, phone number, and authentication records.
  • Profile and company information such as profile photos, company descriptions, team invites, and trust-related details.
  • Marketplace content such as listings, listing images, saved items, order notes, ratings, reports, and messages.
  • Technical data needed to operate the service, including session information, app state, device/browser details, and basic diagnostics.
  • Call-related data required to enable in-app audio calling, such as signaling records and microphone permission state during supported call flows.

How We Use Information

  • Provide sign-in, listings, messaging, calling, and account management features.
  • Improve trust and safety, detect abuse, investigate reports, and enforce marketplace rules.
  • Help users recover accounts, manage support requests, and respond to policy or account questions.
  • Maintain service performance, security, and reliability across web and mobile experiences.

Permissions And Sensitive Access

FarmYard may request device permissions only when the related feature is used.

  • Microphone: for in-app voice calling between users.
  • Photos or media library: for selecting listing images or profile images.
  • Camera: if enabled in a future native build for direct capture.

FarmYard should not access these permissions for unrelated purposes.

Sharing

FarmYard may display profile and listing information that users choose to publish in the marketplace. FarmYard may also share data with service providers that support hosting, authentication, storage, messaging, and app operations. FarmYard does not present itself as a payment processor for marketplace transactions.

Data Retention And User Rights

FarmYard may retain data for as long as needed to operate the marketplace, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce policies. Users should be given a way to request account help, profile corrections, or deletion support through the published support channel before production launch.

Children

FarmYard is intended for general marketplace use and should not knowingly collect personal information from children where prohibited by law.

Contact

For support, email support@modernfarmyard.com or visit our public support page.
