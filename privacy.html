FarmYard

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 15, 2026 | Last updated: April 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how FarmYard collects, uses, stores, and shares information when people use the FarmYard marketplace on web, Android, and iPhone. FarmYard is committed to protecting your privacy while providing a safe, transparent agricultural marketplace.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Full name, email address, phone number, and authentication records
  • Account security details and login history
  • Profile preferences and settings

Profile & Company Information:

  • Profile photos, company names, and business descriptions
  • Company team invitations and access permissions
  • Verification status and trust-related details
  • Business location, registration details, and certifications

Marketplace Content:

  • Listings you create (titles, descriptions, prices, images)
  • Saved listings and preferences
  • Order notes, quantities, and delivery information
  • User ratings, reviews, and feedback
  • Messages and chat history with other users
  • Media files (photos and videos) you upload

Technical & Diagnostic Data:

  • Browser and device information (type, OS, version)
  • Session information and login/logout timestamps
  • IP address and approximate location (for security)
  • App crash reports and error diagnostics
  • Feature usage patterns

Communication Data:

  • In-app voice call metadata (timing, duration, participants)
  • Microphone access permissions for calling features
  • Support requests and correspondence

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Core Platform Features: Sign-in, profile management, posting and browsing listings, messaging, in-app calling, and transaction coordination
  • Trust & Safety: Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and policy violations; investigating user reports; enforcing marketplace rules
  • User Support: Account recovery, password resets, technical support, and answering policy questions
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns, fixing bugs, improving performance, and developing new features
  • Verification: Verifying business credentials and legitimacy for company profiles
  • Communication: Sending important updates, notifications, and information about policy changes
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting legal obligations and responding to lawful requests

3. Data Sharing

We share information only as necessary to operate FarmYard:

  • Public Information: Profile details, listings, ratings, and company verification badges are visible to all marketplace users
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting providers, authentication services, analytics services, and payment processors that support marketplace operations
  • Legal Requests: Law enforcement or government agencies when required by law
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred (you will be notified)

Important: FarmYard does not act as a payment processor. We do not store or transfer payment information. All payments are arranged directly between buyers and sellers outside the platform.

4. Permissions & Device Access

FarmYard may request device permissions only when features that require them are used:

  • Microphone: Required only for in-app voice calls between users
  • Camera: Used only when you choose to take or upload photos for listings or profile
  • Photo Library/Files: Accessed only when you select images to upload for listings
  • Location: Used to show nearby listings and help with local search (can be disabled in settings)
  • Contacts: Never accessed without explicit user action
  • Notifications: Only sent if you enable notifications in settings

FarmYard will never access permissions for unrelated purposes or without clear user consent.

5. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Your information is retained for as long as your account is active
  • After Deletion: Most information is deleted within 30 days of account deletion, though backup copies may be retained for up to 90 days
  • Legal Holds: Information may be retained longer if required by law or for dispute resolution
  • Messages: Chat history is retained until deleted by the user or after 2 years of inactivity

6. Your Privacy Rights

  • Access: You can view all your personal data by logging into your account
  • Correction: You can update your profile, listings, and preferences anytime
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and associated data (except where legally required to keep records)
  • Export: You can request a copy of your data in a standard format
  • Opt-Out: You can disable notifications, hide your phone number, or make your profile private

To exercise any of these rights, contact support@modernfarmyard.com

7. Security

FarmYard uses industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure password hashing and storage
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited employee access to personal data
  • Two-factor authentication options

However, no system is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, change your password immediately and contact support@modernfarmyard.com

8. Children's Privacy

FarmYard is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we become aware that a minor has provided personal information, we will delete it promptly.

9. International Data Transfers

FarmYard uses cloud services that may store data in multiple countries. By using FarmYard, you consent to your information being transferred to, stored in, and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including countries that may not have equivalent privacy protections.

10. Third-Party Links

FarmYard may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Always review their privacy policies before sharing information.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of FarmYard after changes means you accept the new policy.

12. Contact & Support

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, want to exercise your privacy rights, or need assistance:

  • Email: support@modernfarmyard.com
  • Support Page: Visit Support
  • In-App Support: Use the help option in the app menu

FarmYard takes privacy seriously. If we cannot resolve your concern, you may have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
